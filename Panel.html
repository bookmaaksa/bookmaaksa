    Junior Writers' Panel

    Are you passionate about writting, then this is ideal for you as we offer support to beginner writers, hobbyists, students, or those new to professional writing. With an unlimited support and assistance to foundational writting skills and solutions tailored to give you access to a community forum for peer feedback and an opportunity to submit work for light critique. By Joining the Junior Writters' panel you automatical access your gateway to growing your writting career as you unlock the most important thing on your publishing journey such as unlimited book cover design and review for all your works, and a free isbn code for all your successful published books.

    Intermmediate Writers' Panel

    Are you a writer with some experience, a published author seeking for structured growth?. This is ideal for you as we offer you with benefits mainly focused on growing your Portfolio by engaging and developing elements to get your audience effectively. by subscribing to intermediate writers' panel you unlock benefits such as advanced workshops in genre-specific writing, pacing, dialogue, and self-editing. You also get access to a moderated critique group, exclusive interviews with published authors/editors, early access to Bookmaaksa's writing challenges/contests, Unlimited and free professional editing services, And get recruited on research and surveys platforms and get paid.

    professional Writers' Panel

    Profesional writers' panel is ideal for Established writers, freelancers, traditionally published authors, and those wishing to or pursuing full-time writing careers. We have implemented an advanced platform to develop authors as we offer countless benefits on their projects, all authors on profesional writers' panel get an exclusive benefits such as Masterclasses with bestselling authors, agents, and publishers, Aprofessional website to showcase their work and publications, social links and direct purchase links, priority submission opportunities to literary agencies/partners, in-depth manuscript reviews and query letter critiques. networking events (virtual/in-person) with industry professionals. access to a private mentorship program (limited slots). marketing/publishing strategy sessions.
